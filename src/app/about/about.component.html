Sun Tzu's Art of War is the world's earliest known treatise on warfare and military strategy. It is a strategic and tactical guide for ordinary people, business leaders, rulers and commanders. It is considered to be one of the most influential philosophical works in ancient China. Sun Tzu was a Chinese military strategist, Taoist philosopher, and general in the 6th century BC. Sun Tzu's teachings are concise and elegant, simple and philosophical, and extremely practical. The book has been constantly reinterpreted, reinvented, and imitated recently.
